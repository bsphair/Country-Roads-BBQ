About Us

Mountaineer BBQ was founded in 2005 by Scott Phair in Wheeling, West Virginia. Our goal from the very beginning has been providing customers with quality BBQ and exceptional customer service. Crafting and perfecting his own BBQ sauce, Scott began Mountaineer BBQ with his signature item, the pulled pork sandwich. Scott introduced his sandwich at various food and catered events across the region. Over the years, the menu has been expanded to include items such as ribs, baked beans, and brisket. All meats are carefully smoked and cooked the day prior to maximize the BBQ flavor.

Scott Phair, Owner

Scott has over 30 years of restaurant and customer service experience. He enjoys being able to create quality BBQ products and interacting with his customers.

He resides with his wife of over 30 years in Wheeling, West Virginia.

Kelly Phair, Chef

Kelly has over 5 years of customer service and food industry experience. She enjoys baking and fostering local cats who are in need of a home.

She resides in Wheeling, West Virginia.
